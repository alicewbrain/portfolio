Designing Composable DeFi Strategies: Furucombo

In the Web3, the DeFi protocol Furucombo restructures fragmented multi-protocol operations into a visual composable system, allowing complex workflows to be constructed intuitively and executed within a single transaction.

While DeFi is highly capable, most strategies are still script-based or executed through disconnected transactions. This persists simply because the interaction model has not evolved over the years.

At Furucombo, which launched in 2020, I approached this problem by turning DeFi operations into composable visualization actions. Each protocol action, such as Swap, Borrow, or Liquidity Provision, is represented as a visualization. Users can connect these actions to construct a strategy, and the system executes the entire sequence as a single transaction on-chain.

Furucombo showcase by prominent Web3 channel The Defiant

At first glance, this may appear to be a visual interface problem. In reality, the challenge lies much deeper in defining a usable abstraction. The difficulty was not simply designing the interface but shaping an abstraction that could support many possible strategy combinations while remaining predictable for users.

As the sole designer on the product, I worked closely with engineers to develop this interaction model from the ground up; the engineers and I collaborated to define the structure of the system itself.

Once this model was established, new behaviors became possible. Complex strategies could be visually composed and understood, allowing them to be shared and reproduced. This empowered non-engineers to construct operations that previously required writing scripts.

Today, the product has processed tens of billions of dollars in on-chain transaction volume and completed a seed funding round. More importantly, it demonstrates a shift in how DeFi interactions can be structured, moving from isolated operations to composable strategy design.

Scalable Architecture

Defined a robust abstraction layer balancing complexity and flexibility. This programmable framework enables seamless expansion, supporting infinite transaction paths with precision and a design system adaptable to any protocol.

Visual Composability

Transforms complex DeFi actions into intuitive Blocks, making strategy building as easy as stacking Legos. Accessible to non-engineers, this lowers the barrier for no-code strategies and shapes early composable DeFi experiences.

Proven Market Impact

Since its 2021 seed round backed by Tier 1 VCs including Multicoin Capital, Binance Labs, Defiance Capital, 1kx, SevenX Ventures, and Aave founder Stani Kulechov, the platform has driven billions in volume.
