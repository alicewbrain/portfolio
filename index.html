Composable UX

Designing Composable DeFi Strategies: Furucombo

Furucombo launched in 2020. The platform was built to refactor complex, multi-protocol Web3 operations into a composable, visual system. It packages decentralized finance smart contracts and flash loan into modular "cubes," enabling users to 'combo' multi-action strategies and execute them within a single, atomic transaction.

In the design context of Furucombo, while Web3 decentralized finance is immensely powerful, most advanced strategies remained script-based or confined to isolated transactions, simply because interaction models had not evolved alongside the underlying protocols. At first glance, this might seem like a superficial interface problem. The core challenge lay in defining a scalable abstract concept that went far beyond designing a standard UI layout.

At the time, no product in the market offered multi-protocol strategy composition. Users were forced to manually submit isolated actions protocol by protocol, waiting for on-chain confirmation between each transaction and repeating this time-consuming process until all steps were completed. Until this limitation was broken through a workflow architecture that consolidates multiple protocols and actions into a freely composable, single-click transaction workflow, establishing the true framework for strategy composition.

As the sole product designer, I architected a 0-to-1 UX framework, creating a mental model that balances high flexibility with predictability to support near-infinite strategy combinations and dynamic chained states. I translated complex underlying code logic into a modular visual system, designed real-time status displays and error-guidance mechanisms, and collaborated closely with engineers to implement this novel interaction model into a scalable front-end architecture.

Furucombo showcase by prominent Web3 channel The Defiant

This design unlocked entirely new user behavior patterns. It empowered users without coding knowledge to drag and drop multi-protocol actions, including swap, borrow, liquidity provision, and flash loan from platforms like Uniswap, Compound, and Aave among others, into a single transaction flow, formally operationalizing the concept of Composable UX within decentralized finance.

As a strategy-driven design product, it shaped early composable decentralized finance experiences, pioneering a completely new track for Web3 aggregators and defining the interaction thinking framework.

More importantly, it operationalized the visualization of financial Lego's underlying logic, proving that user interactions can seamlessly transition from isolated transactions to holistic strategy design.

The platform ultimately demonstrated the efficiency of on-chain capital flows, driving over $50 billion in transaction volume since securing seed funding in 2021 from Tier-1 VC firms, including Multicoin Capital, Binance Labs, DeFiance Capital, 1kx, SevenX Ventures, BTC12 Capital, and Aave founder Stani Kulechov. Reflecting this innovation, the Furucombo team was invited to present these design breakthroughs at EthCC in both 2022 and 2023.
